Thai Pronunciation Guide for English Speakers

Thai pronunciation differs from English in four big ways: (1) many consonants come in aspirated vs. unaspirated pairs, (2) vowel length (short vs. long) changes meaning, (3) several final consonants are unreleased, and (4) Thai is a tone language with five tones.
